How to Use IP Geolocation Tracker

🔍 Single IP Lookup

Enter any IPv4 address in the search box and click "Search" to get detailed geolocation information.

📍 My IP Address

Click "My IP" to instantly track your own IP address and see your location on the map.

🗺️ Interactive Map

The map view displays the IP location. You can zoom, pan, and interact with the map to explore.

📊 Detailed Information

Switch to the "Details" tab to see comprehensive information about the IP address including ISP, timezone, and coordinates.

📜 Search History

All your IP lookups are saved in the History tab. Click any entry to re-view its details.

⚙️ Batch Lookup

Look up multiple IP addresses at once by entering them in the Batch section.
